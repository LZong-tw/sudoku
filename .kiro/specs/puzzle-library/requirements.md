# Puzzle Library Requirements

## Overview
Defines requirements for puzzle difficulty and clue counts.

## Requirements

### REQ-PUZZLE-001: Easy Puzzles
- **Description**: Easy difficulty puzzles
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - 10 puzzles minimum
  - 38-45 clues per puzzle (47-56% completion)
  - Solvable with basic techniques

### REQ-PUZZLE-002: Medium Puzzles
- **Description**: Medium difficulty puzzles
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - 10 puzzles minimum
  - 28-34 clues per puzzle (35-42% completion)
  - Requires intermediate techniques

### REQ-PUZZLE-003: Hard Puzzles
- **Description**: Hard difficulty puzzles
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - 10 puzzles minimum
  - 22-27 clues per puzzle (27-33% completion)
  - Requires advanced techniques
